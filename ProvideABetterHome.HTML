  1. Assess The Pet's Health and Behavior

    2. Make sure they are in good health, and if you're planning on placing them in our shelter, provide a recent veterinary check-up report that incudes vaccination history, health, spayed/neutered, and free of any medical issues. Be honest about the pet's behavior and any special needs they may have.

  2. Gather Important Information

    3. Prepare a detailed profile of the pet that we'll post, including:

    • Name, breed, age, and sex.

    • Personality traits. (e.g., energetic, calm, friendly)

    • Photos and videos to showcase their appearance and behavior.


  3. Send Us The Pet's Information

    4. We'll prepare a post that includes the pet's name, sex, age, and your phone number if you haven't placed it in the shelter.

  4. Screen Potential Adopters

    5. It's important to find a responsible and caring home for a pet. You have the option of arranging to chat, meet, and visit a potential adopter's house, or we'll report to you about it if the pet was placed in our shelter.

  5. Completing the Adoption

    6. Once a suitable adopter is found, finalize the adoption by:

    1. Completing an adoption contract or agreement to ensure a clear transfer of ownership.
    2. Providing the adopter with the pet's medical records, care instructions, and any necessary supplies.



  6. Follow Up (Recommended)

    7. Consider staying in touch with the new owner, if both parties agree, to check on your pet's adjustment and offer any advice as they settle into their new home.
